Sport's left-back, Igor Cariús is the target of a search and seizure by Operation Maximum Penalty II, launched by the Public Ministry of Goiás (MPGO), which investigates the actions of a criminal organization specializing in the manipulation of sports results in professional football matches. The games under suspicion were valid for the 2022 Series A and in other State this year: Goiano, Gaúcho, Mato-Grossense and Paulista.

That is, the games under suspicion refer to the period in which the athlete was in Cuiabá, and not in Sport, considering that he arrived at Rubro-negro only for the 2023 season. Last year, the athlete played for Dourado in 32 matches.
